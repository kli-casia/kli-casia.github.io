Hello there! I'm Kai Li, currently an Associate Professor at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), working with Prof. Jian Cheng. I received the Ph.D. degree from National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), CASIA in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Weiming Hu and Prof. Junliang Xing. Before that, I received the B. Eng. degree from Dalian University of Technology (DUT) in 2013.

My research interests include Deep Reinforcement Learning, Foundation Models for Decision Making, and AI for Science. In my opinion AGI cannot be achieved without an interdisciplinary approach. My long-term goal is to add some pieces to the puzzle :-) Please do not hesitate to drop me an email if you are interested in working with me.
